## Purpose

Tracks every intent claimed by a topic or URL - at the working title stage and beyond. Check this log before approving any new working title or writing any post. Intent is claimed when a topic is added to the topical map, not when it is published.

**Rule:** One intent = one piece of content. If a proposed title matches an existing fingerprint, do not create a new post - update or expand the existing one.

## How to Write an Intent Fingerprint

Format: [Audience] + [query type] + [context/occasion] + [funnel stage]

Examples:
- "Diner choosing between shareables and mains at decision stage"
- "Group organizer looking for casual pub options for a weeknight gathering at consideration stage"
- "Local resident discovering pubs near downtown Kitchener at awareness stage"

Keep fingerprints specific enough that no two can overlap. If two working titles produce similar fingerprints, they are cannibalizing - merge or cut one.
