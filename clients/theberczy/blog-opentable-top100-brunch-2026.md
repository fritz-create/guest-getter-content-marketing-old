## Blog Content

*As featured on OpenTable's Canada's Top 100 Brunch and Lunch Restaurants for 2026*

OpenTable has named The Berczy one of Canada's Top 100 Brunch and Lunch Restaurants for 2026. For us, this means more than just being listed.

Brunch and lunch at 69 Front Street East has always been about something specific: a meal in Old Town Toronto that doesn't rush. Chef Michael Angeloni's kitchen. A room that makes you want to stay longer than you planned. Live piano on weekends. That's what we've been building. Day by day, service by service, guest by guest.

---

### A Proper Sunday Tradition

Brunch here has become a ritual for a lot of people. And that's what we're most proud of. Not the recognition itself, but what it reflects. The guests who come back weekend after weekend because this has become part of how their Sunday feels.

It's moments like these that matter most to us. Not just recognition, but real connection with the people who walk through our doors. And this Sunday, we have a little more reason to celebrate.

---

### Come Celebrate Mother's Day With Us

Mother's Day is May 10th. We're hosting a special brunch at $59 per person, and we'd love to be the place you bring her.

The piano will be playing. The room will be doing what it does. Whether it's her first time at The Berczy or her tenth, a Sunday morning here is a proper way to say thank you to someone who deserves it.

To everyone who has dined with us, supported us, and made this a habit. Thank you. And if you haven't been yet, Mother's Day is a good place to start.
