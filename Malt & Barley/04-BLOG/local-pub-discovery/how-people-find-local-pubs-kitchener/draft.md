## Like a Proper Local on Fischer-Hallman

Malt & Barley is located inside Williamsburg Town Centre at 1187 Fischer-Hallman Road, near the Max Becker roundabout.

If you are coming from Highway 401, take Highway 8 west into Kitchener and follow Fischer-Hallman south. From downtown Kitchener or Waterloo, Highway 7/8 connects you directly. GRT Bus Route 22 Laurentian West stops right outside, and there is plenty of free parking on site.

McClennan Park and Max Becker Common are both a short walk away if you are making an afternoon of it.

Open seven days a week from 11:30 AM, Malt & Barley has become a go-to Irish pub Kitchener residents rely on for scratch-made comfort food, rotating taps, and weekly events.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where is Malt & Barley located in Kitchener?

Malt & Barley is at 1187 Fischer-Hallman Road inside Williamsburg Town Centre, in the heart of south Kitchener's Williamsburg neighbourhood, near the Max Becker roundabout. Easy to reach from Highway 401 via Highway 8, or from downtown Kitchener and Waterloo via Highway 7/8. GRT Bus Route 22 Laurentian West stops right outside.

### Is there parking at Malt & Barley?

Yes. Free parking is available on site at Williamsburg Town Centre, with plenty of spaces for both quick visits and longer evenings out.

### Does Malt & Barley take reservations?

Walk-ins are always welcome. For groups or private events, we recommend reaching out ahead of time. Our private back room seats up to 30 guests and is available for birthdays, team celebrations, family dinners, corporate events, and more. Call us at 519-745-6460 or visit our Private Events page to check availability.

### What are Malt & Barley's hours?

We are open seven days a week from 11:30 AM. Monday to Thursday until 10 PM, Friday and Saturday until midnight, and Sunday until 10 PM. We are open on most holidays including Family Day, Valentine's Day, St. Patrick's Day, Mother's and Father's Day, and long weekends.

### What events does Malt & Barley host?

There is always something on. Trivia nights, live music, music bingo, and daily specials run throughout the week. Live sports are streamed on 8 big screens with access to FuboTV and DAZN, covering Premier League, Champions League, NFL, NBA, NHL, MLB, Super Bowl, World Cup, and more.
